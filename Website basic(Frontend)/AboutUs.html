     About Medical Hospital

  
 

    Medical Hospital, a private hospital with a public
     purpose, offers a world-class quaternary care facility,
    featuring multiple Centers of Excellence. It brings 
    together the finest medical minds in the country and 
    advanced technology to provide holistic treatment within 
    a multi-disciplinary framework. 
    As the first NABH International (National Accreditation 
    Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers)
    accredited hospital in Nepal, Nepal Mediciti aspires 
    to become the healthcare destination of choice in 
    South Asia. It provides a platform for practicing 
    ‘Safe and Evidence Based Medicine’ driven by 
    international guidelines and protocols.
    The hospital represents the best amalgamation of 
    world-class technology and the best medical minds, 
    ensuring exceptional care for all patients. With a
    capacity of 750 beds, Nepal Mediciti is the largest 
    private hospital offering super-specialty treatment 
    services in Nepal. It boasts well-equipped modular 
    operation theatres with Hepa filters, integrated 
    features, and a Gas Scavenging System, incorporating 
    the latest available technology. These advanced 
    facilities ensure that patients receive the highest
    standard of medical care in a safe and sterile 
    environment.
